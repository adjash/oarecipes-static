1. Preheat the oven, peel the shallots and toss in a bowl with 1 tbsp EVOO + salt and pepper. Place in the oven and roast for 25 ish minutes, or just until they look the way you want.
2. Soften the onions in a pan with oil, after 3/4 mins add the crushed garlic.
3. Once the garlic is fragrant, add the beans (with or without stock), veg stock and lemon zest. Cook until the beans are soft and heated through.
4. Once the beans are soft and heated, remove from the heat and blitz them until smooth in a food processor.
5. Return to the pan and keep on a low heat.
6. Remove the shallots from the oven with roughtly 5 minutes to go, and drizzle with balsamic dessing.
7. To serve, spread the puree into the bowl, attempt to place some shallots neatly on top and garnish with a sauce (something like chimichuri).
