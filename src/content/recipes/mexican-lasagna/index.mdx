1. Preheat the oven at 200°C, heat a frying pan on medium-high heat and spray it with a nonstick spray.
2. Add the diced onion and ground chicken, using a spatula to break apart the chicken. Season with salt, pepper and the spices. Cook until the chicken until brown, stirring often ~10 mins.
3. After the chicken is cooked through, add the tomatoes, corn and black beans. Bring it to a simmer for 5 mins.
4. Using a casserole dish, add a layer of ground-chicken mix, followed by 25g of cheese. Make a layer of triangled tortillas. Top with more of the chicken mix and another 25g of cheese + triangled tortillas. Repeat until the dish is full and finish with a layer of cheese on top.
5. Bake in the oven until the cheese is melted and browned. When removed from the oven, let it set for 5 to 10 mins.
