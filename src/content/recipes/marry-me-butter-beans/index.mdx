1. Heat the oil in a large pan on medium heat. Add the shallots and sun-dried tomatoes and cook until softened.
2. Add the minced garlic and salt, stirring until fragrant for 30 sec or so.
3. Pour the wine in and increase heat to medium-high. Cook until wine is almost fully reduced, around 2 minutes.
4. Add spinach and place a lid on the pan until just starting to wilt, about 2 minutes or so.
5. Add the beans and stock. Cook until the spinach is fully wilted and beans are heated through fully.
6. Remove from heat add the cream and stir. Once fully incorporated add the parmesan.
7. If you have remaining parmesan, sprinkle on top and garnish with fresh basil if you feel so inclined.
8. Serve with crusty bread for dipping in the sauce.
