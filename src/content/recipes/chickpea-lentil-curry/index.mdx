1. In a large pot, add a bit of water and let it bubble, then add the diced onion and sweat until soft.
2. Add another bit of water to prevent onion from sticking. Add the spices and cook for a minute or so.
3. Add passata, maple syrup, chickpeas, lentils and veg stock cubes. Stir to incorporate everything together. Cook for 2 mins.
4. Add the coconut milk, garlic and tomato mixture. Turn the heat down and simmer for 10 mins.
5. Add the spinach leaves, stir through until wilted.
